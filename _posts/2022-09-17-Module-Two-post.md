On completion of this module : - 
<ol>

## Vulnerability Audit and Assessment - Baseline Analysis and Plan
You have been tasked by an organisation to evaluate their website (provided in Unit 1) and produce an analysis document. It should consist of:

Details of possible security vulnerabilities.
A list of standards appropriate to their business and any non-compliance against those standards.
A summary of recommendations and potential mitigations that could be used to ameliorate any risks. These should be ordered by importance.

## Vulnerability Audit and Assessment - Results and Executive Summary
Following your analysis document that you submitted, you should now produce an executive summary that pulls together your findings, recommendations and conclusions in a clear and unambiguous format. It should consist of:
A brief summary of the work carried out.
Summary findings – presented in an easy to understand, non-technical manner
A section that evaluates the website against two security standards – one of which must be the GDPR directive.
Conclusions – with justifications.
Recommendations – with justifications, ordered by business priority.
